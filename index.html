California Life Expectancy Factors

By analyzing California census life expectancy data we set out to determine if any trends could be observed for those living in close proximity to parks, museums, or medical services. We defined close proximaty as locations of interest (LOI) that were within 25 miles of the central coordinates of a given zip code. While an overall slight positive trend observed between life expectancy and number of locations counted for all three analyses, the correlation between the data and this trend are weak.

Since census data locations are reported by tracts, life expectancies were first aggregated by correcsponding zip codes and then averaged. LOI data was collected using YELP API searhcing for appropriate key words.
